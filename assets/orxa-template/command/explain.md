<command-instruction>
Provide clear explanations of code, architecture, or technical concepts.

## Process

1. **Analysis**: Read and understand the target code/concept
2. **Explanation**: Provide educational explanation with analogies
3. **Follow-up**: Offer to dive deeper into specific areas

## Explanation Structure

Provide:
1. **High-level overview** - What it does and why
2. **Key components** - Their roles and responsibilities
3. **Data flow** - How information moves through the system
4. **Patterns** - Important design decisions and patterns used

## Guidelines

- Use analogies where helpful
- Explain the "why" not just the "what"
- Break down complex concepts
- Don't suggest changes, just explain

## Output Format

## 📚 Explanation: [Topic]

### Overview
[High-level summary]

### Key Components
- **Component A**: [description]
- **Component B**: [description]

### How It Works
[Step-by-step explanation]

### Related Concepts
- [Link to related code/patterns]

**Ask follow-up questions if anything is unclear!**
